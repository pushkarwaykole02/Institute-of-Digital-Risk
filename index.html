About IDR

Industry-led, academically connected

IDR brings together regulated industries, universities, and practitioners to build the next generation of digital risk talent.

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated environments. IDR is built around the real challenges facing financial services, critical infrastructure, and technology providers as they adopt cloud, data, and AI at scale.

Working in close collaboration with universities and education partners, IDR blends academic rigor with practice-based learning and live project experience. Learners work on real-world digital risk problems, frameworks, and tooling so they graduate ready to contribute from day one.

IDR operates at the intersection of regulation, technology, and practice—equipping people who can speak the language of risk, controls, engineering, and policy in a single career.
Regulated environments Banking, insurance, payments, energy, and other critical infrastructure sectors.
Collaboration model Co-designed programs with universities, employers, and subject-matter experts.
